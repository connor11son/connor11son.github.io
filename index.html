About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student studying Statistics and Data Science at the University of California, Santa Barbara. I am interested in most things ML, but a lot of my motivation comes from studying computer vision techniques (classification, localization, etc.). I am very interested in "intelligence" in general, and spend a lot of time thinking about my own (and others') definition of the word.

Outside of my own studies, a lot of my time gets spent with the UCSB Data Science club. The Data Science club is a place where data science noobs can learn the basics of coding and data analysis, or where experienced members can work on projects together to learn/strengthen skills demanded in industry.
